What Are Claude Skills?
Claude Skills are instruction packages you give Claude so it does your specific workflows, in your specific way, every time — without you having to re-explain yourself.
Think of them as hiring a specialist: instead of a generalist who needs briefing every time, you get someone who already knows your industry, your formats, and your standards.
Skills 2.0 (released March 2026) adds a Skill Creator, A/B testing, and evals — so you can build, test, and prove your skills actually work.

How to Install
Option 1 — Manual install (Claude.ai)

Copy the SKILL.md file from the skill folder you want
In Claude.ai, go to Settings → Skills → New Skill
Paste the contents and save
